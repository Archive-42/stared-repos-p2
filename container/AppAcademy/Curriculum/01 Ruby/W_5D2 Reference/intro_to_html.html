Description

A delicious Italian treat of multi-colored bell peppers, with your choice of spicy or sweet Italian sausage.

Sausage & Peppers

Ingredients

  • Bell Peppers
  • Sweet or Spicy Italian Sausage
  • Onion
  • Olive Oil
  • Chili Flakes
  • Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Slice Onions and Peppers into strips
  2. Heat Oil in Skillet
  3. Brown the Sausage
  4. Add Peppers and Onions
  5. Season with Chili Flakes and Black Pepper to desired spiciness
