Stevie Wonder - Tribute Page

Singer, Songwriter, Producer, Composer, Musician, Multi-Instrumentalist, Social Activist

Short Bio:

  • Early Life
    • Born May 13, 1950
    • Real name Stevland Hardaway Morris
    • Born in Saginaw, MI
    • Born 6 weeks premature which resulted in blindness
  • Music Career
    • Signed to Motown at 11 years old
    • Producer Clarence Paul gave him the name "Little Stevie Wonder"
    • Wrote multiple hits for himself as well as others while at Motown
    • Sixties singles: 1962–69
    • Seventies albums and classic period: 1970–79
    • Commercial period: 1980–90
  • Legacy
    • 30 US Top-Ten Hits
    • 25 Grammy Awards (The most ever by a solo artist)
    • Won a Lifetime Achievement Award
    • Inducted into the Rock and Roll/Songwriter's Hall of Fame
    • Sold over 100 million records
    • Influenced countless artists (including me) as well as genres of music
  • Personal Life
    • Married to Syreeta Wright 1970-1972
    • Married to Kai Millard 2001-2012
    • Nine children with 5 diffferent mothers
